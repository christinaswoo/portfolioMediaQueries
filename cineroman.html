Expectations

“Expectations” is a ciné-roman short film inspired a rupi kaur poem from her book, Milk and Honey. to demonstrate kaur’s message of the importance of self-love, classic shots such as Times Square, taxicabs, and skyscrapers of New York City were contrasted with calm, indoor shots to represent how assumptions about people will often differ from how one feels about themselves.
